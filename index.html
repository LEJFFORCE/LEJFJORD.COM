About Jesper

I've been in both rooms.

I've led teams through 32-billion-kronor procurement processes. I've built companies from zero to 12M — twice. I've been COO, founder, NGO-builder, and the guy who figured it all out on the outside.

I've also screamed at the people I love. Hidden behind my calendar. Fought my own shadows for years. Done the real work — in men's circles, somatic therapy, gestalt, constellations — and found my way through.

I'm not a therapist. I'm not a life coach. I'm a man who has lived what you're living — and found a way through it that didn't require choosing between your business and yourself.

That's what I bring to this work.
